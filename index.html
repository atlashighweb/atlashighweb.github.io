OUR VISION

 

Atlas High School! Our institution is dedicated to providing students with a holistic education that not

only focuses on academics but also nurtures their talents and skills. Here at Atlas High School, we offer

 the Ugandan curriculum with an emphasis on both education and co-curricular activities.

OUR MISSION

To provide quality education to all interested students irrespective

of religion, tribe or nationality

OBJECTIVES

To provide quality education to a wide cross section of parents.

To provide a suitable and conducive learning environment.

Attain a high level of performance at both ‘o’ and ‘a’ Levels of education.

To encourage and facilitate students’ participation in the curriculum implementation.

To tap and nature students’ talents.

To produce students with leadership skills.

To instill a sense of self-discipline, responsibility and harmonious co-existence.
