HOLIDAY WORK

At Atlas high school, we recognize the importance of balancing academic learning with personal time, especially during the holiday season. While holidays are an excellent opportunity for students to recharge and enjoy time with family and friends, we also encourage them to keep their minds active and engaged with a variety of meaningful tasks and projects.

Our Holiday Work assignments are designed to be flexible and engaging, allowing students to apply what they’ve learned while encouraging creativity and independent thinking. These assignments are crafted to be manageable and enjoyable, ensuring that students can continue their academic progress without feeling overwhelmed during their break.
